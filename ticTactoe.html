This game is for two players. One player is Red (X), the other is Blue (O). The goal is to get three boxes in a row (across, down, or diagonally). When you enter your letter into box and click OK, the box will change to your color. The game ends either when one player wins or there is a tie. Note: Default color is blue for anything other than "X".
